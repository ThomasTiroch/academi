At Heroes School we believe that the key aspects to our success will lie in;

Ability to attract and retain a competent team of professional and devoted teachers Perception in the marketplace/stakeholders as a Center of academic excellence as well as moral and social nurture for the children at our service. Establishing and sustain trust with our potential clients; learners and community as a private education center committed to their cause. Creating an attractive, safe, secure and inclusive learning environment.

Since starting operations in **2009** with a population of **95 pupils**, we have grown to over **350 pupils** and have a dedicated team of **28 staff members**. Our facilities include **13 classrooms**, a kitchen, and separate latrines for boys and girls.
