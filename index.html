Executive Summary

Heros Academy is a Mixed Nursery, Primary, and Junior Secondary School located in **Shanzu, Mombasa County, Kenya**. We aim to be a successful and sustainable educational center that provides affordable, high-quality, comprehensive education to children, especially those from low-income households. Our approach is driven by social inclusion, creating a conducive environment that embraces social, religious, and cultural diversity while supporting every learner to achieve their full potential.

Since starting operations in **2009** with a population of **95 pupils**, we have grown to over **350 pupils** and have a dedicated team of **28 staff members**. Our facilities include **13 classrooms**, a kitchen, and separate latrines for boys and girls.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

A community where all children have an equal opportunity to access quality education services for sustainable development.
