Sustainability & Environmental Education

At our school, we believe in preparing learners not only for exams but also for life. Thats why environmental protection and sustainability are at the heart of our curriculum.

  • Soil Conservation & Development : Learners study soil health, erosion control, and regeneration methods.
  • Sustainable Skills : From solar energy to renewable resources, students gain hands-on experience in modern sustainability practices.
  • Practical Projects : Each grade takes part in real-life applications. For example,here our Grade 8 showcased creative ways of conserving soil through exhibitions and demonstrations.
  • Green Responsibility : By engaging in these projects, learners build a mindset of responsibility toward nature, community, and future generations.

    • Participating in Class

    Our learners are encouraged to take an active role in lessons. Participation includes asking questions, sharing ideas, respecting others opinions, and contributing positively to group work. Through active participation, students develop confidence, communication skills, and a deeper understanding of the subject matter.

  • Active Engagement Ask/answer questions and stay focused.
  • Collaboration Work well in groups and share ideas.
  • Respectful Behavior Listen without interrupting.
  • Preparedness Bring materials and complete homework.
  • Contribution Add useful points to discussions.
  • Critical & Creative Thinking Analyze, compare, and justify ideas with evidence.
